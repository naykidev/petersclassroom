# Wix App Market Listing — AccessFlow

## App Name

**AccessFlow — Accessibility Toolbar**

## Short Description (160 characters max)

Free accessibility toolbar for every visitor. Font, contrast, spacing, and motion controls. One click to install, zero configuration.

## Long Description

Give every visitor the accessibility controls they need — without any code or configuration.

AccessFlow adds a floating accessibility button to every page of your Wix site. Visitors click it to open a panel where they can adjust:

### FONT & TEXT

- 5 font options including OpenDyslexic and Atkinson Hyperlegible for dyslexia and low-vision readers
- Text size scaling from 80% to 200%
- Letter spacing, word spacing, and line height
- Text alignment (left, center, justified)

### CONTRAST & COLOR

- Dark mode
- High contrast black-and-yellow
- Grayscale, sepia, and inverted color filters
- Link highlighting with yellow background

### READING AIDS

- Reading guide bar that follows the cursor
- Underline all links
- Highlight all headings
- Keyboard navigation helper
- Enhanced focus indicators

### MOTION & CURSOR

- Reduce motion and animations
- Pause all animations
- Large cursor for motor and low-vision users

### HOW IT WORKS

Install AccessFlow from the Wix App Market. The toolbar appears on your site immediately — no setup, no code, no configuration. Each visitor's settings save automatically in their browser.

### CUSTOMIZE FROM YOUR DASHBOARD

Open the AccessFlow dashboard to change the button position (bottom-right, bottom-left, top-right, top-left), pick an accent color, and show or hide specific features.

### WHO IS IT FOR

- Schools and districts serving students with learning differences
- Nonprofits and community organizations serving diverse audiences
- Healthcare providers and clinics
- Any business that wants to signal that their site is built for everyone

Completely free. No monthly fees. No limits.
