Hiring that starts with accommodation.

Most job platforms make you disclose what you need and hope for the best. Axol Work flips it around. You tell us how you do your best work once, and every shift you see is ranked by how well it fits. Employers say up front what they offer. No guessing, no awkward asks, no barriers hiding in the fine print.

Who it's for

Built for people who work differently.

Whether you're looking for shifts or looking to hire, Axol Work is designed so accommodations are the starting point, not an afterthought.

Seekers

Neurodivergent and disabled workers looking for shift work that respects how they operate. Set your accommodation needs once, browse shifts sorted by fit, apply in a tap, and build a verified work history that speaks for you.

Employers

Businesses that want an inclusive, reliable workforce. Post shifts with the accommodations you offer, review applicants, and confirm work history so great workers get the credit they've earned.

The community

Beyond jobs, Axol Work is a professional network. Connect with peers, join community groups, find a mentor who shares your background, and message people directly. Careers grow through people, not just postings.

For seekers

Find work that fits.

Four steps from sign-up to shift, with your needs leading the way.

1

Set how you work

Pick your experience, availability, and accommodation needs, quiet workspace, seated breaks, written instructions, flexible starts. It takes a couple of minutes and you only do it once.

2

See shifts ranked by fit

Every open shift shows a fit badge like 3 of 4 needs met so you know at a glance which ones actually work for you. Filter by city and browse without the guesswork.

3

Apply in a tap

One tap to apply. Track every application, submitted, viewed, accepted, from a single screen, and message the employer directly with any questions.

4

Build a verified history

Ask employers to confirm the jobs you've done. A verified badge turns your track record into proof, so the next opportunity comes easier.

For employers

Hire people who are ready to work.

Post a shift, meet applicants, and build an inclusive workplace, all from one dashboard.

01

Post the shift and the support

Title, pay, time, and the accommodations you offer, headphones allowed, seated stations, structured training. Seekers who fit find you first.

02

Review applicants side by side

See everyone who applied across all your shifts. Open a profile, message them, and accept or decline, without a pile of email threads.

03

Close the loop

Mark an accepted shift complete with hours worked. Clean records for you, a growing verified history for the worker.

04

Earn a reputation

Verified employees can leave tag-based reviews, break policy clear, accommodations honored, so inclusive employers get recognized for it.

Accessible by design

Not a feature. The foundation.

Axol Work meets WCAG 2.1 AA, because a platform for disability-inclusive hiring has to work for everyone who uses it.

01

Full keyboard & screen reader

Semantic structure, visible focus, and live announcements for new messages, notifications, and status changes. Everything works without a mouse.

02

Light, dark & your zoom

A proper dark mode, respect for your system preference, and layouts that hold together when you scale text or zoom the page.

03

Calm by default

Motion is gated behind your reduced-motion setting, targets are large and easy to hit, and color is never the only signal, status always pairs with text and icons.

04

Private & respectful

Your email stays out of your public profile. Block anyone, report content, and control who sees your posts, connections only or everyone.
