How it works

How it works.

1

Download AccessFlow

Click the download button below. You get a zip file. No account, no email required, no strings attached. Just a free plugin.

2

Upload to WordPress

In your WordPress dashboard go to Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin. Select accessflow.zip and click Install Now. Takes about ten seconds.

3

Activate

Click Activate Plugin. The AccessFlow toolbar appears on every page of your site immediately. Nothing to configure.

4

Visitors take control

A floating accessibility button appears on your site. Each visitor adjusts it to their needs — font, contrast, spacing, motion. Their settings save automatically and persist across visits.

Features

Everything visitors need.

Font & Text Size

Five font options including OpenDyslexic, Lexend, and Atkinson Hyperlegible. Text scaling from 80% to 200%. Helps readers with dyslexia, low vision, or cognitive differences.

Contrast & Color

Dark mode, high contrast black-and-yellow, grayscale, sepia, and inverted colors. One click changes the whole site instantly.

Spacing Controls

Letter spacing, word spacing, and line height — all independently adjustable. Visitors set exactly what helps them read and focus.

Motion & Reading Aids

Reduce or pause animations, enhance focus rings, underline all links, highlight headings, enable a reading guide bar that follows the cursor, and enlarge the cursor itself.
